About

Aaron Anglin is a results-driven accountant based in Philadelphia, PA, with a robust background in financial reporting, tax systems, and team leadership. Holding a B.S. in Accounting from Arizona State University, Aaron has built a career bridging technical accounting expertise with innovative system implementations.

Throughout his career, Aaron has held key leadership positions, including Treasurer and Fundraising Chair for nonprofit organizations, where he managed budgets exceeding $500K and led successful fundraising initiatives. His technical proficiency spans enterprise systems including FAST GenTax, iasWorld, Workday, and ArcGIS, complemented by extensive integration experience with 45+ vendor systems.

Beyond his professional achievements, Aaron is deeply committed to community service. He volunteers with local charities and enjoys an active lifestyle that includes hiking and cycling. A passion for photography allows him to capture the beauty of both urban landscapes and natural environments.
